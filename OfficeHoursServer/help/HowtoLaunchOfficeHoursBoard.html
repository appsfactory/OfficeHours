How to Launch Office Hours Board

Getting Started ››

The Administrator or technical staff must go to OfficeHours/boardActivation.php and then they will find the Office Hours  Board Activation and choose both Branch and Location in the Local Office Hours Board dialogue box.


Then Press "Launch" and the Office Hours Board will appear with Name, Office Hours, Branch, and Location.  If the User is in their Office, then a green icon appears before their name.  If the User is not signed in or signed out, then a yellow icon appears before their name.  Finally, if the User has no Schedule, then a red icon appears before their name.
